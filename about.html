About Me

Hi, my name is Yoshua Elmaryono. You can call me Joe. I was born in Indonesia. I'm proficient in both Bahasa Indonesia and English.

I believe in the power of habits. And so, I made a personal commitment to always live near the edge of my capabilities until the day that I die. As of 2018, this commitment has enable me to do 10 repetitions of one armed push up (5 reps each arms) and to learn enough about programming autodidactically to get paid doing it.

My favorite hobby is reading. I also enjoy learning things. I've even overcome one of my weakness (not good enough social skill) in high school, simply by reading books (and blogs), and practicing what I learned there in real life.

Nowadays, I have good enough social skill to work well together with colleagues that have different belief systems and characters.

While some of our weakness can be overcome, some can only be accepted. For me, my weakness that I've accept is that I'm a highly functioning schizoid (NOT schizophrenic). The highly functioning part means that I don't have trouble working and playing with others (they might even come to me for my skill at work or because they cherish me personally). The schizoid part means that I prefer being mostly alone.

Running is my favorite sport since I can do it alone. Singing and listening to music are my favorite way to have my simple joy in life. Watching online videos and reading books (manga, biography, etc.) are what I do when I want to waste a bit of my time.
